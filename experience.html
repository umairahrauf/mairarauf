This is my experience.


So this is my experience as an intern at the Melaka Public Library Corporation for 2 months. This experience taught me to be an independent person and enhanced my ability to adapt to new environments. During my two-month internship at the Melaka Public Library Corporation, I was exposed to various tasks that helped me grow both personally and professionally. I learned how to manage my time effectively, prioritize tasks, and work efficiently under minimal supervision. This role required attention to detail, especially when organizing materials and assisting patrons, which honed my organizational skills and taught me the importance of precision in a professional setting.
